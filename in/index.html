Do You Know the Rules?

What are the features of the insurance?

  • • You can enroll if you are between 18 and 74 years and 364 days old.
  • • Protection in case of death due to illness or accident is provided up to 99 years and 364 days.
  • • You can freely choose the beneficiaries of your Life Insurance, as long as there is an “insurable interest”.
  • • It allows automated payment methods: SIP.
  • • There is flexibility in the protection amount you desire. The minimum is 1.5% of the total accumulated savings in the insurance.
  • • You can make up to 12 partial withdrawals per year starting from the 3rd month of the policy. (1)
  • • Once you enroll in your Protected Investment Savings Insurance, you can unlock 6 additional investment options in your Client Zone.
  • • A fixed monthly administration fee is charged, in addition to a cost for the death coverage that varies according to the age and gender of the insured, regardless of the accumulated amount.


What situations are not covered by the insurance?

  • The insured’s participation in international or civil war.
  • Suicide, self-harm, or self-injury.(2)
  • Death penalty.
  • Participation in any criminal act.
  • A criminal act committed as an author or accomplice, by a beneficiary or anyone who may claim the insured amount or indemnity.
  • War, invasion, acts by foreign enemies, hostilities, or military operations.
  • Engaging in a sport or activity related to or on account of work, which the parties have agreed to exclude from coverage, due to the policyholder not accepting a premium surcharge.
  • Any information that was not declared by the insured at the time of policy enrollment or during its validity, and therefore unknown to the insurer.
  • Nuclear fission or fusion, or radioactive contamination.
  • Any illness or health condition that was diagnosed or known by the insured before the policy enrollment.

(1) Any tax on withdrawals or policy payouts will be borne by the client.

(2) The Insurer will pay the insured capital to the Beneficiary if death occurs as a result of suicide, provided that two full uninterrupted years have passed since the insured's enrollment in this policy.
